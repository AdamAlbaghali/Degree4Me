Performing Arts Degree

About Performing Arts

The Performing Arts degree offers comprehensive training in various aspects of performance, including acting, dance, and music. Students develop their skills in performance techniques, stagecraft, and artistic expression.

Required Classes

  • Acting Techniques
  • Dance Performance
  • Vocal Training
  • Theater History
  • Stagecraft and Production
  • Script Analysis
  • Musical Theater
  • Improvisation
  • Directing
  • Senior Performance Project
