Business Degree

About Business

The Business degree program provides students with a broad understanding of business principles and practices. It covers areas such as management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship, preparing students for diverse career opportunities.

Required Courses

  • Introduction to Business
  • Principles of Management
  • Financial Accounting
  • Marketing Management
  • Business Law
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Operations Management
  • Business Ethics
  • Strategic Management
  • Entrepreneurship
