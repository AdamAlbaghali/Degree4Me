Fashion Design Degree

About Fashion Design

The Fashion Design degree program focuses on developing creativity, technical skills, and industry knowledge necessary for a career in fashion. Students learn about fashion illustration, garment construction, textile design, and fashion marketing.

Core Courses

  • Fashion Drawing
  • Pattern Making and Draping
  • Textile Design
  • Garment Construction
  • Fashion History
  • Fashion Merchandising
  • Visual Merchandising and Display
  • Fashion Marketing and Branding
  • Portfolio Development
  • Internship in Fashion Industry
