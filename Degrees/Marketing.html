About Marketing

The Marketing degree program focuses on understanding consumer behavior, market trends, and promotional strategies essential for businesses to succeed in competitive markets. Students develop skills in market research, branding, advertising, and digital marketing.

  • Brand Management
  • Digital Marketing

  • Social Media Marketing
  • Marketing Analytics
