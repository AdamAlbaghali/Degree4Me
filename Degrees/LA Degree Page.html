Liberal Arts Degree

About Liberal Arts

The Liberal Arts degree offers a broad education covering various subjects in the humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences. It focuses on critical thinking, communication skills, and a well-rounded understanding of the world.

Required Classes

  • Introduction to Philosophy
  • English Composition
  • History of Western Civilization
  • Introduction to Psychology
  • Principles of Sociology
  • Mathematics for Liberal Arts
  • Introduction to Political Science
  • Introduction to Fine Arts
  • Foreign Language Studies
  • Senior Seminar in Liberal Arts
