Dentistry Degree

About Dentistry

The Dentistry degree program focuses on training students to become qualified dental professionals capable of providing oral healthcare services to patients. It covers areas such as dental anatomy, oral pathology, dental surgery, and preventive dentistry.

Core Courses

  • Dental Anatomy and Physiology
  • Oral Histology and Embryology
  • Restorative Dentistry
  • Oral Pathology
  • Periodontology
  • Orthodontics
  • Endodontics
  • Oral Surgery
  • Pediatric Dentistry
  • Public Health Dentistry
