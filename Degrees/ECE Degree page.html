Early Childhood Education Degree

About Early Childhood Education

Early Childhood Education focuses on the education and development of young children, typically from birth to around eight years old. This degree program prepares students to work as educators or administrators in preschools, daycare centers, and other early childhood settings.

Required Classes for the Degree

  • Child Growth and Development
  • Curriculum Planning for Early Childhood Education
  • Child Psychology
  • Early Literacy Instruction
  • Health and Nutrition in Early Childhood Education
  • Childhood Behavior Management
  • Observation and Assessment in Early Childhood Education
  • Family and Community Engagement
  • Practicum in Early Childhood Education
