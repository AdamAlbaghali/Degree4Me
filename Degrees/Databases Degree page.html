About Databases

The Databases degree focuses on the design, development, and management of databases. It covers various aspects such as database modeling, SQL programming, data warehousing, and database administration.

Required Classes

  • Introduction to Databases
  • Database Design and Management
  • SQL Programming
  • Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence
  • Database Administration
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Data Mining
  • Advanced Topics in Databases
  • Internship in Database Management
  • Senior Project in Databases
