Degree in Engineering

About Engineering

Description: This degree program covers a wide range of topics in a general engineering degree, including Mechanics, General Chemistry, and Electrical Engineering

Required Courses

  • Calculus
  • Differential equations
  • Linear algebra
  • General physics
  • Mechanics
  • Thermodynamatics
  • General chemistry
  • Engineering mechanics
  • Engineering ethics
  • Electrical engineering

Mechanical Engineering: Mechanics of Materials, Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Mechanical Design

Electrical Engineering: Circuits and Electronics, Signal Processing, Power Systems

Civil Engineering: Structural Analysis, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering

Chemical Engineering: Chemical Thermodynamics, Transport Phenomena, Process Control

Aerospace Engineering: Aerodynamics, Aircraft and Spacecraft Design, Propulsion
